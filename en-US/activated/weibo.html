Congratulations, Weibo is now activated in Firefox!

  1. Share content you like through Weibo

    2. With the click of a button, you can Share interesting content on any website with your social network through Weibo.

  2. Add the Sidebars button to your toolbar

    3. Starting with Firefox 30, you can manage your sidebars using the Sidebars toolbar button. The Sidebars button is located in the customization palette, just drag the button to your toolbar for fast switching between sidebars.

    Additionally, you can easily change the service in your sidebar, or hide the sidebar, from the sidebar itself. You can also use the View->Toolbars menu in Firefox.

  3. Change Settings from the Add-ons Manager

    4. You can easily manage your services, disabling them temporarily or removing them, by viewing the Add-ons Manager in Firefox.
