Firefox SocialAPI Demo Instructions

To install from this directory you must modify the social.directories preference in Firefox to include the domain , you can do that in about:config.

Demo Social Service

Congratulations, Demo Social Service is now activated in Firefox!

  1. Get the most from the Demo Social Service service

    2. Bookmark this page

    If you're new to this service, bookmark this page, it contains useful information and links to further help using and customizing Firefox. Keep reading to lean more about how the Demo Social Service service works in Firefox.

  2. Managing your Demo Social Service sidebar

    3. Starting with Firefox 30, You can manage your Sidebars using the Sidebar toolbar button. You can find the Sidebar toolbar button in the customization palette, just move the button to your toolbar for fast switching between sidebars.

    You can easily change the service in your sidebar, or hide the sidebar, from the sidebar itself. You can also use the View->Toolbars menu in Firefox.

  3. Share content you like through Demo Social Service

    4. With the click of a button, you can Share interesting content on any website with your social network through Demo Social Service.

  4. Save content for later

    5. With the click of a button, you can save interesting content on any website to Demo Social Service.

  5. Keep an eye on the latest from Demo Social Service

    6. Demo Social Service will provide you with the latest from their service, just click on their icon in the toolbar.

  6. Change Settings from the Add-on Manager

    7. You can easily manage your services, disabling them temporarily or removing them, by viewing the Add-ons Manager in Firefox.

  7. Next Steps

    • Learn more about what this service adds to Firefox.
    • Learn more about what Demo Social Service provides.
    • Explore and find more services you can add to Firefox.
